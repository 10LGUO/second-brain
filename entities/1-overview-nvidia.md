# NVIDIA

NVIDIA is the dominant manufacturer of GPU hardware for AI training and inference, and the developer of the CUDA software ecosystem. In the context of AI infra, NVIDIA sets the de facto standard that all other hardware and software must follow or be compared against.

## Why It Matters to This Wiki

NVIDIA's hardware and software stack form the primary reference environment for all AI infra work discussed in this wiki. Domestic (non-NVIDIA) chip vendors are explicitly described as operating in a **follower state**, adapting their frameworks and tools to match NVIDIA's feature set. NVIDIA's QA processes and ecosystem scale are cited as the reason GPU precision is reliable.
