# AI Infrastructure Stack for Large Models

AI infrastructure (infra) for large models refers to the full system of hardware, software, frameworks, and engineering practices required to train and serve large language models (LLMs) and related deep learning models efficiently and correctly. The core axes of concern are **compute, storage, communication, precision, and performance**. The goal is to reduce training and inference costs while maintaining model correctness.
